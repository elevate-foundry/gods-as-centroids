# Real Embeddings Experiment — Braille Bottleneck on Religious Texts

## Design

**Scorer:** `anthropic/claude-sonnet-4` scores 24 canonical religious passages on 12 theological axes.
**Traditions:** Aboriginal Australian, Buddhism, Christianity, Daoism, Greek, Hinduism, Islam, Judaism, Lakota, Norse, Zoroastrianism
**Bottleneck:** 72-bit braille lattice (6 bits × 12 axes)
**Baselines:** Random binary projection (72 bits), PCA + uniform quantization (72 bits)
